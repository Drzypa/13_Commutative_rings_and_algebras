In this entry, we will derive the properties of derivatives of polynomials in a rigorous fashion. We begin by showing that the derivative exists.

Let A be a commutative ring and let p be polynomial in A⁢[x]. Then p′ is the unique element of A⁢[x] such that p⁢(x+y)=p⁢(x)+y⁢p′⁢(x)+y2⁢r⁢[x,y] for some r∈A⁢[x,y]
