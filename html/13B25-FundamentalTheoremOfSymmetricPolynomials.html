fundamental theorem of symmetric polynomials


Every symmetric polynomial  P⁢(x1,x2,…,xn)  in the indeterminates x1,x2,…,xn can be expressed as a polynomial  Q⁢(p1,p2,…,pn)  in the elementary symmetric polynomials p1,p2,…,pn of x1,x2,…,xn.  The polynomial Q is unique, its coefficients are elements of the ring determined by the coefficients of P and its degree with respect to p1,p2,…,pn is same as the degree of P with respect to x1.
