UFD’s are integrally closed


Theorem: Every UFD is integrally closed.

Proof: Let R be a UFD, K its field of fractions, u∈K,u integral over R. Then for some c0,…,cn-1∈R,

un+cn-1⁢un-1+…+c0=0

Write u=ab,a,b∈R, where a,b have no non-unit common divisor (which we can assume since R is a UFD). Multiply the above equation by bn to get

an+cn-1⁢b⁢an-1+…+c0⁢bn=0

Let d be an irreducible divisor of b. Then d is prime since R is a UFD. Now, d|an since it divides all the other terms and thus (since d is prime) d|a. But a,b have no non-unit common divisors, so d is a unit. Thus b is a unit and hence u∈R.
