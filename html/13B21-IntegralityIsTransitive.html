integrality is transitive


Let C⊂B⊂A be rings. If B is integral over C and A is integral over B, then A is integral over C.

Proof. Choose u∈A. Then un+b1⁢un-1+⋯+bn=0,bi∈B. Thus C⁢[b1,…,bn,u] is integral and thus module-finite over C⁢[b1,…,bn]. Each bi is integral over C, so C⁢[b1,…,bn] is integral hence module-finite over C. Thus C⁢[b1,…,bn,u] is module-finite, hence integral, over C, so u is integral over C.
